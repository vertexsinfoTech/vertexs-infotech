Proof of Concept (POC) Development for Startups

Have you ever had a brilliant idea that you were excited to pursue, only to discover that it wasn’t feasible or didn’t work as expected? This happens with all the startups but there is one solution: Developing a Proof of Concept (POC). It allows entrepreneurs like you to test their innovative ideas before investing significant resources into development. With a POC, you can develop a scaled-down version of your product to test its core features and functions, gather feedback from potential customers, and refine your product roadmap. It’s like a “trial run” for your idea, providing valuable insights into your product and its potential success in the market. 

At Codevian, we provide POC Development for startups to make their startup product ready for the market. We help you test your idea at an early stage, so you can identify potential issues and areas of improvement. This will let help you develop a more effective product strategy, and ultimately increase your chances of success as a startup. So, if you have a brilliant idea, don’t hesitate to explore it with a POC – who knows, it might just be the next big thing!

The Journey from POC to MVP Development for startups

Development of Proof of Concept (POC)

We help you determine your idea’s feasibility and whether it’s worth investing more time, money, and resources into developing a full product

Modeling and Designing

After POC development, we refine the design, identify issues and gather feedback from users to inform further development.

Rolling out Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

We develop a stable product with core features, collect user feedback, and aim to deliver value and meet user needs in designing and launching an MVP.

Our Strategic Process for Your IT Solution

This ten-step plan provides a framework to build a successful solution for your small business and startup, from ideation to continuous improvement, while ensuring that the product meets the needs of the target audience and delivers value to the market.

1.Identifying the problem

We identify the problem you want to solve and ensure that there is a demand for a solution that your startup idea will offer.

2.Conducting market research

Our team conducts market research to understand your target audience, competition, and market trends.

3.Developing a business plan

We develop a business plan that outlines your goals, target market, pricing strategy, and revenue model while considering all aspects.

4.Creating a (POC)

Our team then creates a proof-of-concept (POC) to test the feasibility of your solution and gather feedback from users.

5.Designing a prototype

Once POC is created, we move forward to create a prototype based on the feedback we receive from the POC.

6.Development of MVP

When everything is on track, we develop a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) with core features that resonate with the target audience.

7.Testing and Product QA

We continuously test and refine the product considering the feedback received from the users to improve its functionality.

8.Launching the product

Once everything is ready, we launch your product in the market and monitor its performance to identify areas for improvement.

9.Scaling the produc

Once your product is successful and stable, we scale it by expanding the target market or adding new features.

10.Continuous improvement

Your startup product development doesn’t end here. We continuously improve the product based on customer feedback and market trends to help you gain a competitive edge and meet evolving customer needs.

How can startup IT services help me with my business?

What is the process for developing a startup idea into a product?

The process typically starts with ideation and workshop sessions to refine the idea and create a roadmap. This is followed by proof-of-concept (POC) development, prototyping, and MVP development. After launch, ongoing support and maintenance are provided to ensure the product’s success.

How do I know if my idea is viable and worth investing in?

Startup IT services can help you conduct market research and validate your idea to determine if it has potential. They can also provide guidance on how to improve your idea to make it more viable and attractive to investors.

How long does it typically take to develop a product from ideation to launch?

The timeline can vary depending on the complexity of the product and the scope of work. However, the typical timeframe is around 3-12 months from ideation to launch.

Our Process

At Vertexs, we take the time to deeply understand our customers' needs and requirements. We then craft tailored plans to effectively execute these objectives, ensuring every detail is aligned with the client's vision and goals for a seamless experience.

Discussion & Brainstorming

For us each idea/requirement shared is equally important, team takes this very seriously and work together to discuss all aspects with the client. Notes taken from call and boards gets filled with discussions

Strategy & Prototyping

As clients express an interest in doing business with us, our expert strategists begin the work process. Once everyone in the team has an agreement on an idea, all of us immerse ourselves in the project prototyping

UI/UX

To us, design is much more than just the aesthetics. It's about the functionality. We want your users to have the best experience with your digital business, so we focus on making user experience intriguing, addictive, intuitive and friendly

Implementation

Experienced and competent developers at Skycap take the lead and pair the right technology for a perfect solution

Testing

We put the whole developed plan into action by creating a beta version of product. Each module of the project is scrupulously tested and goes through multiple testing phases under various environments and conditions to ensure the best result

Launch & Support

Our experts deploy the end product on respective app stores and web browsers, assuring the passing of the fickle approval process and the standards of the online stores. We provide support for any bugs or questions coming from the customers.
